Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Read how we protect your information.

Last updated: 2026

Any Browser Android App ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Any Browser does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or payment details.

We may collect limited non-personal information including:

  • App usage data (pages visited, features used)
  • Device information (device model, Android version)
  • Crash and error reports (for app improvement)

How We Use Information

The collected information is used only to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Enhance user browsing experience

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Cookies and Web Data

Any Browser is a web browser application. Websites you visit may use cookies. Cookies are managed by the respective websites and not by Any Browser.

Users can clear browsing data and cookies anytime from the app settings.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob (for displaying ads)
  • Google Analytics (for app usage analysis)

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

Permissions

Any Browser requests only necessary permissions:

  • Internet access – for web browsing
  • Storage access – for downloads (optional)

Children's Privacy

Any Browser does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect user information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
